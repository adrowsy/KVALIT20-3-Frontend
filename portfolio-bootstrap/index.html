Accessibillity testing

Test object: Swedish municipal web diary

Accessibillity testing based on the Act on Accessibility to Digital Public Services with a special focus on documents (.pdf) distributed via the website. The law has been in force since 1 January 2019 for all public websites in the EU. The scope has been adapted to the time limits when different parts of the Web Accessibility Directive must be met.

In summary

  • Individual project, 20 hours
  • Focusing on capability, reliability och usability limited to WCAG A-levels 1.1.1, 1.3.2, 1.3.1 and 1.3.4
  • Deliverables: STP, STD, STC, STCR, STR, SDR, STS

System testing

Test object: Fashion e-commerce site

Student assignment involving system, regression and re-testing of e-commerce site to be carried out according to the test process (analysis, design, execution and report) learned throughout the course.

In summary

  • Group project, 170 hours in total
  • Focusing on capability, reliability and compatibility for functions of search and filter products, shopping cart and check out
  • Deliverables: STP, STD x 2, STC x 2, STCR x 2, STR x 2, SDR, STS

About

Annika Rengfelt, born in cold winter 1986, lives in Sundbyberg, Stockholm.

I've loved the internet since I was twelve and learned HTML from a book. I think my first website was about horses.

Now training to be a specialist in testing and quality assurance. Especially interested in simplicity and availability since I believe that available content is better for everyone and that all people should be able to enjoy great services.

Proficient in Word, Excel and PowerPoint.

Professional experience

  • InsureSec a company offering licensing and regulatory services to Swedish insurance brokers
  • E-commerce marketplace Groupon Sweden
  • Sales rep @ Statoil gas station (now Circle K) where I learned tons about providing efficient and excellent service to many types of people (who haven't visited a gas station, right?)

Education

  • HVE programme for software testing and quality assurance @ Nackademin
  • Bachelor programme in social anthropology @ Stockholm University
  • Courses in philosohy @ Södertörns högskola
  • Two-year education in printmaking arts @ Grafikskolan (College of Printmaking Arts)
